Hivelight

Group light shows & music sync for iPhone

What is Hivelight?

Hivelight is a social light-show app. Connect several iPhones in the same room and they flash together as one — rainbow splits, waves that ripple across the group, stadium effects and more. Add music sync that pulses your phones to the beat, and a DJ-style set builder to choreograph the whole night.

Features

Crowd SyncConnect nearby phones for a synchronized group light show — no internet needed
Music SyncBeat-accurate light pulses driven by the music around you
DJ Set BuilderChain scenes with timing to choreograph an entire party
