Hivelight Party

Turn every phone in the room into one synchronised light show.

For iPhone, iPad & Apple Watch · iOS 17+

What it is

Hivelight Party links up to 8 iPhones over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi into a single light show. Open a room, share a 6-digit code, and the room reacts to the music together — auroras, bass drops, strobes, the works. No accounts, no servers, no internet required.

Free to join · Pro to host

Guests always join free. To host a room, subscribe to Hivelight Pro — monthly, yearly (best value) or one-time lifetime. 7-day free trial on subscriptions. Manage or cancel anytime from your Apple ID settings.

Safety

Hivelight Party contains intense flashing lights and strobe effects. Do not use if you have photosensitive epilepsy or any seizure disorder. Take breaks during long sessions — strobe modes warm the device. Keep volumes moderate so the mic can hear the beat without distortion.
