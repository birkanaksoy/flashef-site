Privacy Policy

Effective: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flashef iOS application (the "App"), developed by Birkan Aksoy, handles user information.

1. Data We Collect

None. Flashef does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, usage data, analytics, identifiers, or device information.

2. Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone only when you use the "Music Sync" effect. The microphone signal is processed locally on your device in real time to estimate ambient audio level. The audio is never recorded, stored, written to disk, or transmitted anywhere. Microphone access is released as soon as you stop the effect.

3. Camera / Torch Access

Flashef uses the camera framework solely to control the LED torch for flash effects. The camera sensor is not activated and no images or video are captured.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

Flashef saves the following data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults:

  • List of presets you marked as favorites
  • Onboarding completion flag
  • Counter for the in-app review prompt

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

5. Third-Party Services

Flashef does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Flashef does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact abirkan.aksoy@gmail.com.
